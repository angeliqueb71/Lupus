2017 WALK TO END LUPUS NOW, NYC

On Saturday, May 6, 2017, join the Walk to End Lupus Now™ New York City and help us reach each the 10-year milestone of $4,000,000! Join with thousands of others impacted by lupus walking to recognize those living with lupus, honor those we’ve lost, and fulfill our vision of a life free of lupus. Since 2008, New York City Walk participants have raised over $3.25 million and walked a combined total of nearly 100,000 miles to fund education and support programs, advocacy initiatives, and research to improve the quality of life of the nearly 1.5 million people living with lupus in the United States.
